Defender of the Relam

September 2022

In a land where nothing is simple and fair is a myth, there is a man who never tires. As the legend goes, this man was born from a bloodline that carries unlimited strength and remarkable appearance. He is known to disguise himself as a commander of a group of humans performing services to those in need of repair to their carriages. It is told that should he be discovered as a god it would destory the sprits of humanity, and those who have wronged him would painfully perish. As the prophecy goes, there will but one woman that can harness his power. There is limited knowledge of this womans identity, but she is said to be a powerful sorceress who can conjure relams from nothing.

Cure Loneliness with Alchmey

September 2022

After almost 404 failed attempts I have finally done it! I have created a spell so powerful it will one day cure loneliness accross the relam! Using the powers SQLAlchemy I have created a way to teleport messages and information to a safe place that can be later used to connect my people. The excitement is shortlived however, now that I have harnessed this power I need to ensure this powers is used for good. There are other sorcerers that use this power as dark magic and even a means of mind control. I have to be careful not let this happen in my relam.
